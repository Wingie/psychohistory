WHAT THE SIX STATES MEAN (each has a historical face)
ARRIVEDyou cross the transition first, intact, and set its terms. Britain and the railway age.
CONVOYthe pack arrives together because knowledge was shared or standardized; the gains without the wreck. Rail gauge standardization, open protocols, FOSS. Once everyone knows the secret of fire, nobody has an advantage, and everybody has fire.
SHOTGUNsomeone else crosses first and you live in the world they chose. Tokugawa Japan and Perry's black ships.
HELDnobody crosses; the transition is refused, and you pay in everything it would have bought.
CRASHEDa corner taken beyond your control authority. Chernobyl.
PILEUPthe coupled crash: everyone leaning on everyone, everyone down together. The Late Bronze Age; 2008 with balance sheets instead of bronze.
